Hi I am Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a fictional character and the protagonist of J.K. Rowling's series of fantasy novels. He is a young wizard who discovers his magical heritage and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Throughout the series, Harry faces various challenges and battles against the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who seeks to conquer the wizarding world. The books have been immensely popular worldwide and have been adapted into successful films, making Harry Potter an iconic figure in popular culture.
